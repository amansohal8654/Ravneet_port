Our Infrastructure

Our company owns a state-of-art infrastructure that is equipped with all the requisite tools and techniques to make us carry out our business operations in a sophisticated manner. Our infrastructure consists of a systematic segregation of various units like manufacturing, quality testing, research & development, and warehousing. It is spread over a vast area and is capable of meeting even bulkiest of client orders.

Our manufacturing unit has following machines and equipments:

  • Cutting machine

  • Lathe machine

  • Special purpose machine

  • Material handling equipment


Our manufacturing plant is also equipped with an advanced L&T & Haitian Make Machines,equipped with micro-processor based technology with a capacity of 60 to 150 tons.These Machines Are connected with automatic conveying and dehumidifying air drying facilities.



We are blessed with high-tech infrastructural facility that leverages on contemporary technological advancements.Our vast infrastructure is compartmentalized into various sections such as manufacturing, quality testing, packaging and R&D. Furthermore, the fabrication unit of our organization is installed with modern facilities vital for smooth and hassle free performance of our products. All our departments work in close coordination in order to achieve the pre-determined goals of the organization.

Customization Facilities

  • We have dedicated lines for the assembly of moulded products. Checking & Quality Control of the assembled products can be done simultaneously.

  • We have expertise in manual assembly, ultrasonic welding, screen printing and Inserting Operations to reduce overall assembly costs and provide customer with high quality Finished Products.

  • We have full engineering support for product development, design assistance from mould development stages, through the finished part moulding, assembly & packaging.

Product Packaging

  • Packing of 3 Pin Top Plugs available in Master box and Corrugated box

  • Standard Packing 20 Nos. for 6A Three Pin Top Plug

  • Standard Packing 10 Nos. for 16A Three Pin Top Plug
